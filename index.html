ALittle About The Villa

A Swahili Rustic Haven in Malindi

Welcome to HH Villa Malindi, where traditional Swahili architecture meets modern comfort. Our rustic six-bedroom Villa captures the authentic coastal charm with its makuti roofs and coral stone walls.

The property features three charming bungalows, each with a cozy kitchenette, and a magnificent three-bedroom main house that embodies the spirit of coastal living. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens, our beautiful spa, serene pool, and traditional Swahili-style lounging areas create the perfect retreat.

Experience the warmth of coastal hospitality with flexible booking options for either the entire villa or individual units.
