Our beautifully designed main house offers a perfect blend of luxury and comfort for families and groups seeking a memorable coastal getaway. Frequently Asked Questions

Main House FAQs

How many people can the Main House accommodate?

The Main House comfortably accommodates 6 adults in three spacious bedrooms. Additionally, we can accommodate up to 3 children with extra bedding arrangements (additional charges apply for children above 18 years). Each bedroom has its own en-suite bathroom for privacy and convenience during your stay.

What kitchen facilities are available in the Main House?

The Main House features a fully-equipped kitchen with modern appliances including a refrigerator, gas stove, microwave, coffee maker, water dispenser, and essential cookware and utensils. There's ample counter space for meal preparation and a dining area that can seat up to 8 people. Guests can prepare their own meals or request a private chef service for an additional fee.

Are meals included with the Main House booking?

No, meals are not included with the Main House booking. The house is self-catering with a fully equipped kitchen for meal preparation. Guests are responsible for their own meals during their stay. Upon request, we can arrange for a private chef to prepare local and international dishes for an additional charge, or recommend nearby restaurants and food delivery services.

What is your pet policy?

We welcome pets upon request. If you wish to bring your pet, please let us know at the time of booking. You will need to complete a separate pet agreement form prior to your arrival, and certain conditions may apply. We ask that pets are well-behaved, do not disturb other guests, and are kept off furniture and beds. Additional cleaning fees may apply.

Is the Main House air-conditioned?

The Main House features stand fans in all rooms and large open windows designed for natural ventilation with the coastal breeze. The traditional Swahili architectural design helps maintain comfortable temperatures throughout the house. The high ceilings, makuti (palm leaf) roofing, and coral stone walls naturally regulate indoor temperatures even during warmer months.

Is there additional cost for extra guests?

Yes, there is an additional charge for guests beyond the standard 6-person occupancy. Children over 18 years are considered adults for occupancy purposes. Please inquire about our current rates for extra guests when making your booking. We recommend specifying the exact number of guests (both adults and children) at the time of booking to ensure proper arrangements.
