Spa Questions

Spa FAQs

Do I need to book spa treatments in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking spa treatments at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season. Same-day appointments may be available, but cannot be guaranteed. You can book through our online form, WhatsApp, or by speaking with our staff during your stay.

What makes the Traditional Swahili Massage unique?

Our Traditional Swahili Massage incorporates authentic coastal Kenyan techniques passed down through generations. It combines deep pressure, gentle stretching, and local aromatic oils made from coconut, clove, and cinnamon to release tension and improve circulation. This signature treatment offers both physical relaxation and a cultural experience unique to the Swahili coast.

What should I wear during my spa treatment?

Our therapists are professionally trained to ensure your privacy and comfort. You may undress to your level of comfort, and we provide disposable underwear for body treatments. During massages, you'll be properly draped with towels, exposing only the area being worked on. For facials and beauty treatments, we recommend wearing comfortable, loose clothing.

Are your spa products natural and locally sourced?

Yes, we prioritize natural, locally-sourced ingredients in our spa treatments. Many of our oils, scrubs, and wraps incorporate traditional Kenyan elements like coconut oil, Swahili spices, and indigenous plants. Our body scrubs use local sea salt and organic honey. We're committed to sustainable practices that support local communities while providing authentic, effective treatments.

Can I use the spa facilities if I'm not staying at HH Villa?

Yes, our Garden Spa welcomes outside guests. Non-residents can book individual treatments or spa packages. Advanced reservations are required for non-staying guests. We also offer day packages that include spa treatments and access to certain villa facilities like the pool for a complete wellness experience.

What health conditions should I disclose before a treatment?

Please inform us of any health conditions, allergies, injuries, or if you're pregnant before your treatment. Important conditions to disclose include high blood pressure, heart conditions, recent surgeries, skin allergies, and pregnancy. This information helps our therapists customize your treatment for safety and maximum benefit. A consultation form will be provided before your session.
