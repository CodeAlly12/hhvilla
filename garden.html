Private Garden Bungalows

Our three garden bungalows offer the perfect blend of traditional Swahili charm and modern comfort. Each unit is thoughtfully designed with makuti roofs and coral stone walls that capture the authentic coastal feel while providing all the modern amenities you need.

With private entrances and personal kitchenettes, these single-bedroom retreats are ideal for couples or solo travelers seeking a peaceful getaway. Each bungalow features a comfortable bedroom, an ensuite bathroom with hot water shower, and a small kitchen area for simple meal preparation.

Step outside onto your private terrace and enjoy the views of our lush tropical gardens while sipping your morning coffee or evening drink. The shared swimming pool and lounge areas are just steps away.

Bungalow Questions

Garden Bungalow FAQs

What amenities are included in each Garden Bungalow?

Each Garden Bungalow features a comfortable king-size bed, private en-suite bathroom with hot water shower, kitchenette for simple meal preparation, free Wi-Fi, stand fan for cooling, and a private terrace overlooking the tropical gardens. Guests have access to the shared swimming pool and all common areas within the villa property.

Can the bungalows accommodate more than two people?

Garden Bungalows are designed primarily for 2 adults. However, we can accommodate one additional child under 12 years with an extra bed for an additional charge. Due to space limitations, we cannot accommodate more than 2 adults and 1 child in each bungalow. For larger groups, we recommend booking multiple bungalows or considering our Main House.

How far are the bungalows from the beach?

Our property is located approximately 800 meters from Malindi Beach, about a 10-minute walk. The route to the beach is straightforward, passing through a safe residential area. We can arrange transportation to the beach upon request, or many guests enjoy the short walking distance as part of their daily routine.

Are meals included with the bungalow booking?

No, meals are not included with the bungalow booking. All bungalows are self-catering only and come equipped with a kitchenette for meal preparation. Guests are responsible for their own meals during their stay. There are grocery stores within walking distance, and we can recommend nearby restaurants and food delivery options if desired.

Is the pool private for bungalow guests?

The swimming pool is shared with all guests staying at HH Villa Malindi, including those in the main house if it is occupied. However, our property is designed to provide plenty of space and privacy for all guests. If you desire complete exclusivity, we recommend booking the entire villa property to ensure private use of all facilities.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers from Malindi Airport for an additional fee. Please let us know your flight details when booking so we can make the necessary arrangements. We also offer transportation to other destinations in and around Malindi, including day trips to nearby attractions. Just inform our staff of your transportation needs.
