Beachfront Living

Modern Apartments with Ocean Access

Your Gateway to Coastal Living

Discover our exclusive collection of four beachfront apartments, each offering direct access to Malindi's pristine coastline. These modern units combine contemporary comfort with traditional coastal charm, making them perfect for extended stays, vacation rentals, or permanent residence.

Whether you're looking for a romantic one-bedroom retreat or a spacious two-bedroom family unit, our apartments offer the ultimate in beachfront living. Each apartment features modern amenities, private terraces, and unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean.

Our two-bedroom apartments are available for extended rental stays, while our one-bedroom studios offer both rental and purchase opportunities for those seeking to own a piece of Malindi's coveted coastline.

Why Choose Our Beach Apartments

Each apartment features contemporary furnishings, full kitchens or kitchenettes, high-speed Wi-Fi, and all modern conveniences.

Extended Stay Options

Perfect for longer stays with weekly and monthly rates available. Ideal for remote workers, retirees, or extended vacation.
